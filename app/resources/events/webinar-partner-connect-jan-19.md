## Overview
Join our webinar to learn how to start loading your data from various data sources into Snowflake with just a few clicks. With Partner Connect, a new workflow for Snowflake users, you can easily set up Fivetran data connectors through the Snowflake UI for effortless data replication. The new feature aligns with the Fivetran commitment to reduce the obstacles normally encountered with ETL.
